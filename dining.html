Best Dining Experiences in Duluth: Top Restaurants to Try

Savor fresh seafood, craft beers, and the best of Duluth's culinary scene along the shores of Lake Superior

Top Restaurants in Duluth

Discover the best dining experiences Duluth has to offer, from casual cafes to upscale restaurants.

Duluth Grill

Farm-to-Table • Local
$$ • Moderate

Popular local restaurant known for fresh, locally-sourced ingredients and creative comfort food. Great atmosphere for families and food enthusiasts.

Alto Pino

Italian • Upscale
$$$ • Upscale

Upscale Italian restaurant offering authentic cuisine with a modern twist. Perfect for special occasions and romantic dinners.

Va Bene

Italian • Cozy
$$ • Moderate

Cozy Italian cafe featuring wood-fired pizzas, fresh pasta, and authentic Italian flavors. Family-friendly atmosphere with open kitchen.

Northern Waters Smokehaus

Smoked Fish • Local
$ • Budget-Friendly

Local favorite specializing in smoked fish and sandwiches. Known for Lake Superior whitefish and artisanal smoked meats.

Fitger's Brewhouse Brewery and Grille

Brewery • Historic
$$$ • Upscale

Historic brewery in a beautifully restored building with craft beers, fine dining, and Lake Superior views. Perfect for special occasions.

OMC Smokehouse

BBQ • Smokehouse
$$ • Moderate

Authentic barbecue smokehouse featuring slow-smoked meats, traditional sides, and craft cocktails. Great for meat lovers and BBQ enthusiasts.
