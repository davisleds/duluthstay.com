Duluth Neighborhoods & Attractions

Canal Park

Duluth's premier waterfront destination featuring the Aerial Lift Bridge, Lakewalk, restaurants, and shopping. Perfect for families and couples.

  • Aerial Lift Bridge
  • Lakewalk
  • Great Lakes Aquarium
  • Vista Fleet Cruises

Downtown

Historic downtown with theaters, museums, and shopping. Close to the Duluth Entertainment Convention Center and local businesses.

  • Duluth Entertainment Convention Center
  • NorShor Theatre
  • Duluth Art Institute
  • Local Shopping

Lakeside

Residential neighborhood with easy access to Lake Superior, parks, and local attractions. Great for extended stays.

  • Lakeside Park
  • Lake Superior Zoo
  • Enger Tower
  • Local Restaurants
